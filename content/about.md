Welcome to my website, you might wonder what this person has to offer that various other people already don't. You are absolutely right, i don't have anything different from others. The only place i will differ is the path i am traveling. To be honest, i have no idea how others started their journey towards red teaming or the attackers/hackers path, mine started wayyy back when i was kid when i didn't know what i was doing.


The greatest advantage of a kid is their curiosity, thank god, i was filled with un-ending curiosity(and still). We all know the game Subway Surfers. In the dawn of its existence i was a fan of it, cheap dopamine, highly addictive. But as we know it was pay-to-win, which was a bummer. With a conservative dad who is against casual or heck any kind of gaming(classic), there was no way for me to spend in-game. But i asked what if, whaattt iffff, i could get my hands on the paid stuff and not pay for it. I switched off the internet(or not i don't really remember well, i am sorry) in the phone, opened the game, went to purchase keys, cancelled out of the purchase hoping something happens. Well to my surprise i was able to purchase them without actually purchasing them, thus became my first exploit. Clueless what happened, but happy it happened.


Now with time i was not allowed to game a lot, parental lock, system lock, authentication everywhere made me sick of it. But when you are close to someone you will have immense knowledge about there habits and practices. Once i saw how greasy screen reveals the things you type and voilà i got out of parental lock and system lock, shoulder surfed(didn't know that is what i had done) when they were working through their email, unlocking access to laptop and more gaming.


Days with no knowledge and pure intention to break out of my shackles came to an end during my 10th std schooling days where me and my CS teacher had a conversation of me wanting to break into clash of clans(blud thought he owned the world) and get free gems, i mentioned about the subway surfers exploit and how i got access to stuff which was locked away by parents, and that gem of a teacher opened my eyes towards the world of cybersecurity and hacking. That is how i got into hacking.... well there is one more instance i am leaving out.


Who knew i would get hacked too, lol, welp long story short in order to get free units in the game marvel contest of champions, i gave my credentials to a "genuine" website which will give units for "free".  That is when i truly got into hacking, learnt how devastating it is to get hacked as i lost few of my progression in game, started to learn about great hacks like that of the NSA hack, The anonymous group, yada yada. And i decided to learn more and keep feeding my curiosity which eventually landed me into a pentester role, working at a startup as a cyber security specialist.


Now i have a fleet of certificates started from PNPT and Google Cybersecurity Professional to CPTS, CRTA, AD-RTS and now OSCP/OSCP+  and a lot more in pipeline because i like to catch 'em all. I aiming to be as knowledgeable as an APT as they fascinate me the most and keep me motivated and reminding me of why i chose this path. 


Well what i am going to bring to this world through this blog, i have always wanted to start a website just for the fun of it, now with the market and how we get jobs it seems this has been termed as learning in public, so with this website/blogs of mine i would like the world to know my perspective, my methods of how i approach systems and exploit vulnerabilities. It is for everyone who aspires to become a red teamer, maybe find fun candidate to recruit who would bring flavor to your team.
